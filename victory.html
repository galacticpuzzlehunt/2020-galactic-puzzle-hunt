Wake Up

WAKE UP!? Does that mean what you think it means? Was this whole adventure cooked up by your subconscious? You start freaking out.

Calm down, you tell yourself. Treat it like a puzzle: how can you tell if you’re asleep?

If you had a top and spun it, would it spin forever if you’re dreaming? Or is that just in Inception? Only one way to find out… You think you have a top in your bedside drawer.

You scramble up the stairs to your bedroom, taking them two at a time, when you trip—

and fall.

and fall…

and FALL...

You jolt awake, breathing rapidly and grasping for your phone on the nightstand. You just had the strangest dream. There were puzzles and dots and microscopes and vectors and eyes… So many eyes… You try to remember specific details, but it’s like trying to cup water in your hands—the memories fade away from your mind.

The brightness of the phone screen hurts your eyes, but eventually you can make out the time: 3:14 AM. Wait… 2021?! What happened to the 2020 Galactic Puzzle Hunt? Did you somehow miss it by an entire year?

Or… did it ever really exist at all?
