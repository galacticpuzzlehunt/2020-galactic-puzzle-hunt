Guess Hint Puzzle

Solution: Thrifty/Thrifty
Answer: BABY IMPS

Written by Ashley Kim, Dai Yang, Patrick Xia

This puzzle is presented as a gacha game, where solvers are able to pull random characters out of a fictional gacha. Initially the options are to pull 1x or 10x, but once solvers have seen each of the 48 possible characters at least once, an additional option to pull 100x is added.

Upon being summoned from a pull, each character will be accompanied by a quote as well as a string of stars. The quote is always separated into two lines, with the second line being a crossword-style clue whose answer length is the number of stars. Furthermore, a collection indicates the number of times each character has been pulled.

After pulling a few times, solvers can notice that the characters all come from popular gacha game franchises, with some characters are more rare than others (e.g. Puzzle and Dragons characters appear more often than Fire Emblem Heroes characters), and that the collection is sorted in decreasing order of appearance. Now that we have the constellation names, we need to "sort pity by rarity." As indicated by the flavor text, the gacha in this puzzle has a pity system whereby every 10th pull is from a smaller pool of characters. There are 8 such characters in total, one for each Genshin Impact character, and their rarity ordering can be obtained directly from earlier steps. In decreasing order of probability, they are:
