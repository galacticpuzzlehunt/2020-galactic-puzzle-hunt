Solution: Eye Chart
Answer: FORESIGHT IS 2020

Written by Brian Chen, Jakob Weisblat, Abigail Caron

Each of the answers is tightly related to a phrase of the form “EYE OF <X>”:

  • BEAUTY: eye of THE BEHOLDER
  • CALM: eye of THE STORM
  • DOCTOR STRANGE: eye of AGAMOTTO
  • FERRIS WHEEL: eye of LONDON
  • GALLIFREY: eye of HARMONY
  • MACBETH: eye of NEWT
  • ONE-DOLLAR BILL: eye of PROVIDENCE
  • ONLY CONNECT: eye of HORUS

The <X>’s have distinct enumerations, which fit into the blanks. In order:

  • (L)ONDO(N)
  • (T)HE STOR(M)
  • (A)GAMOT(T)O
  • (N)E(W)T
  • (H)ORU(S)
  • (P)ROVIDEN(C)E
  • THE (B)EHOL(D)ER
  • H(A)RMON(Y)

The provided eye chart contains the letters of the alphabet and the ten digits each exactly once.

5
9Q
JM7
4EUV
WRPK1
HTISA8
G3XD20B
NOFL6CYZ

Each pair of letters extracted above is near each other in the chart, with one or two characters between them. Reading those characters in order, from the first circled letter to the second, gives:

  • L (FO) N
  • T (RE) M
  • A (SI) T
  • N (GH) W
  • H (TI) S
  • P (S2) C
  • B (02) D
  • A (0) Y

The answer is FORESIGHT IS 2020.
