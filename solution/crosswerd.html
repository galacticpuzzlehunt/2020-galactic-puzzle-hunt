Solution: Crosswerd
Answer: HORRIBLE

Written by Jakob Weisblat

We're presented with a crossword whose clues are badly misspelled. If we try solving some of the clues, a lot of them don't seem to fit in the grid, but the misspelled clues suggest that we can also try misspelling answers before entering them. This presents a challenge, because it means we can't be sure of most of the crosses. Generally, the misspellings we must enter in the grid are approximately phonetically correct, which can help with crosses even if we're not exactly sure of some letters.
