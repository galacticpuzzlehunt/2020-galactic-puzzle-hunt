Solution: False Flags
Answer: SKULL AND CROSSBONES

Written by Rahul Sridhar

Each “flag” is composed of multiple stripes of flags from real countries (some vertical, some horizontal) plus a silhouette of a symbol. Each stripe corresponds to a substring of the letters of the country it is from, and all the stripes of a country together in order spell the full country’s name.

Concatenating the letters of the stripes of the new flags produces a word or phrase that is represented by the symbol on it.
