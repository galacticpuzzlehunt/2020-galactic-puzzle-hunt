Menu

Here at the Galactic Diner, we offer a full English breakfast.
Toast Our toast is excellent though burnt in select spots. We're trying to tell the toaster manufacturer about the burn issue, but they keep ignoring our telegraphs.
Eggs From the outside all the way to the yolk in the middle, our eggs have a different taste at every layer.
Pancakes We had two chefs working on the pancakes. Unfortunately, the second chef seems to have a habit of forgetting an ingredient. Our sincerest apologies.
Waffle The waffle decorator seems to have been playing minesweeper rather than pouring syrup on your waffle, but you should know if you try to decorate it yourself that he's careful not to pour the syrup diagonally when he decorates, and to keep the syrup from spilling over between the four quadrants of the waffle.
Fries Across the board, our fry cook has been down to innovate in the world of fries. We think once you've figured out what to fill into each of the 16 spots in the fry, you'll be diagonally excited!
